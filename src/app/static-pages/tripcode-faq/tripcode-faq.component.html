Tripcodes

Tripcodes are a way to differentiate yourself from other users. They are a way to have a unique identifier without needing to create an account. They are generated by taking a password and hashing it with a cryptographic hash function. The result is a unique identifier that can be used to identify you across different threads.

To use a tripcode, simply enter your tripcode password in the "Tripcode Password" field when creating a new thread or comment. If you don't enter a password, a tripcode will be generated for you.

Tripcodes based on easy to guess passwords are not secure. If you want to ensure that your tripcode can not be spoofed, use a strong password that cannot be guessed easily.
