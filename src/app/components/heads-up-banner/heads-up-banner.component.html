Welcome to an app I haven't named yet

This is a love letter to the build thread. Those elusive and detailed threads that used to dominate the internet in the years past. This app is a place to share your projects, your builds, your creations, for free.

No ads, no tracking, no selling your data. Just a place to share your work with the world.
No grifters, influencers, or brand ambassadors. Just real people with real projects.

We don't store user accounts, be as anonymous as you want. In order to differentiate yourself from others, you can learn how to use tripcodes
